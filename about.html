About PromptImageLab

PromptImageLab is a professional reference platform dedicated to AI image prompt standardization.

We focus on creating structured, reusable prompts for:

  • Anime avatars
  • Photo-based AI portraits
  • Manga and cartoon styles
  • Stylized AI character generation

What We Do

PromptImageLab does not generate images. Instead, we provide clear, tested prompt frameworks that work across popular AI image generation tools.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make AI image creation more predictable, repeatable, and accessible for creators, designers, and professionals.

We continuously improve our prompt library based on real-world usage and best practices.
