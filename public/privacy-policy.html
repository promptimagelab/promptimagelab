Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

PromptImageLab is a content reference platform. We collect minimal data:

  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics 4 (GA4). This includes page views, session duration, and traffic sources. No personally identifiable information is collected by us directly.
  • Contact form submissions: If you use our contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Google Analytics: Sets cookies to understand how visitors use our site. Data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Google AdSense: Displays relevant advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to our site or other sites. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4 — Site analytics. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — Advertising. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — Hosting and CDN. See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — Typography. May log requests. See Google Fonts Privacy FAQ.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties beyond the services listed above. We do not build user profiles or engage in behavioural advertising beyond what Google AdSense does automatically.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry, typically no longer than 90 days. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics' standard retention settings (14 months by default).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights or ask questions about our data practices, contact us at promptimagelab@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

PromptImageLab is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when we do so. Continued use of PromptImageLab after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Privacy inquiries: promptimagelab@gmail.com
