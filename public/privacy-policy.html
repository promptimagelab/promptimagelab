Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026  •  Effective: January 1, 2026

PromptImageLab ("we", "us", or "our") operates promptimagelab.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Usage / Analytics Data: Page views, session duration, traffic sources, browser type, device type, and referring URLs — collected automatically via Google Analytics 4. No personally identifiable information is collected by us directly through analytics.
  • Contact Form Submissions: If you use our contact form, we receive your name, email address, subject, and message. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: See Section 3 below.
  • Log Data: Our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may automatically log IP addresses, request timestamps, and user agents for security and performance purposes.

We do not collect payment information, create user accounts, or store passwords.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Understand how visitors use our site to improve content and user experience
  • Respond to contact form inquiries within 24–48 hours
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Monitor site security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze content performance to produce better AI prompt guides

We do not use your information for email marketing, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond those listed above.

3. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics 4): Sets _ga, _ga_* cookies to distinguish visitors and measure site traffic. IP addresses are anonymized. Data is aggregated and does not identify individuals. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-Out.
  • Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense): Google may set cookies via AdSense to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history across sites in the Google Display Network. You can control ad personalization via Google Ads Settings or opt out via Network Advertising Initiative.
  • Essential Cookies: Cloudflare sets technical cookies (__cflb, __cf_bm) required for the site to function securely and efficiently. These cannot be disabled without affecting site performance.

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics 4 — Website analytics. Google Privacy Policy  |  GA4 Data Privacy
  • Google AdSense — Contextual advertising. Google Advertising Policy
  • Cloudflare — Hosting, CDN, and security. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Typography (Inter font). Loaded from Google servers; may log requests. Google Fonts Privacy FAQ

5. Google AdSense & Advertising Disclosure

PromptImageLab displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. We are participants in the Google AdSense program and earn revenue from advertisements displayed on this site.

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for interest-based advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

6. Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We share information only as described in Section 4 (Third-Party Services) for the purpose of operating and improving the site, and only to the extent necessary for those services to function.

7. Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for up to 90 days to respond to inquiries, then permanently deleted.
  • Analytics data: Retained per Google Analytics default settings (14 months). Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely.
  • Server logs: Retained by Cloudflare per their data retention policies (typically 30 days for error logs).

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Object / Opt-Out: Object to processing of your data for advertising purposes.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a portable copy of your data (EU residents).
  • California Rights (CCPA): California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale (we do not sell data).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personally identifiable information, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe we may have collected such information should contact us at [email protected].

By using our site, you consent to the processing of your information in the United States, India, and other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers.
