Frequently Asked Questions

Does PromptImageLab generate images for me? +

No. PromptImageLab is a prompt reference and education platform. We provide the exact text prompts — you paste them into an AI image tool like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion to generate the actual images. We teach you to fish rather than fishing for you.

Are the prompts free to use commercially? +

Yes, completely. All prompts in our library are free to use for personal and commercial projects with no attribution required. The images you generate are subject to the terms of service of whatever AI platform you use to generate them.

Which AI tool produces the best portrait photos? +

For realistic professional portraits: Midjourney v6 with --style raw. For anime avatars: Midjourney --niji 6 or Stable Diffusion with anime-specific checkpoints. For commercial-safe work: Adobe Firefly. For free unlimited generation with maximum control: Stable Diffusion XL with Realistic Vision checkpoint. Each of our guides includes platform-specific syntax and parameters.

Can I generate an AI portrait that looks like me specifically? +

Yes. Midjourney's --cref parameter lets you provide a reference photo URL and will attempt to match your facial structure while applying any style from our prompts. Stable Diffusion's DreamBooth and LoRA fine-tuning on 15-30 of your own photos produces the most accurate personalized results. We cover both techniques in our detailed platform guides.

How many variations should I generate before choosing? +

At minimum 10-20. AI generation has significant output variance, and the best image in a batch of 20 is consistently and substantially better than the first output. Small prompt modifications — changing one lighting term or style reference — can produce dramatically different results. Never use the first generation as your final choice.
