About PromptImageLab

PromptImageLab is a professional AI image prompt reference platform designed to standardize photo-based AI image generation workflows.

Our Mission

We provide structured, proven AI image prompts that work across all major AI tools including Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, and Adobe Firefly. Our prompts are photo-first, identity-preserving, and optimized for social media display pictures.

What We Are Not

PromptImageLab is not an AI image generator. We do not generate, store, or process any images. We are purely an educational reference platform for AI image prompt engineering.
