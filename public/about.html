About PromptImageLab

A professional AI image prompt reference platform built for creators who want consistent, high-quality results from AI image generation tools.

🎯 Our Mission

PromptImageLab exists because writing effective AI image prompts is genuinely difficult, and the gap between a vague prompt and a precise one is enormous. We bridge that gap by providing battle-tested, structured prompt frameworks organized by use case, style, and platform.

Every prompt in our library has been tested across Midjourney, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion. We document what works, explain the reasoning behind each element, and update the library as AI models evolve.

🌐 What We Cover

📸 Instagram DP

Natural portraits, artistic styles, B&W, golden hour — optimized for circle crop display.

🎨 Anime Avatars

Studio Ghibli, cyberpunk, shonen, shoujo — specific studio styles, not generic anime.

💼 Professional Portraits

CEO headshots, LinkedIn portraits, corporate team photos — authority and trust.

✨ Platform-Specific

Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Discord, TikTok, WhatsApp — each with different needs.

🔮 What We Are Not

PromptImageLab is not an AI image generator. We do not generate, process, host, or store any images. We are a text-based educational and reference resource.

To use our prompts, you need a subscription or account with an AI image tool such as Midjourney, DALL-E via OpenAI, or Stable Diffusion via your own hardware or a cloud provider.

👑 Who Uses PromptImageLab

  • Content creators and influencers upgrading their social media presence
  • Entrepreneurs and founders building a professional personal brand
  • Game players and community members wanting unique Discord or gaming avatars
  • HR and communications teams generating consistent corporate portrait sets
  • Anime fans wanting high-quality custom avatars in their favourite style
  • Anyone who wants a better profile picture without expensive photography
