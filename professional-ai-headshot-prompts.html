Professional AI Headshot Prompts

Use these professional AI headshot prompts to create clean, corporate, high-quality profile pictures for LinkedIn, resumes, portfolios, and business websites.

Best Professional Headshot Prompts

Corporate Studio Headshot
Clean · Corporate
Create a professional corporate headshot from my photo. Place the subject in a premium photography studio with a neutral office-style background in soft grey or warm taupe. Use classic three-point studio lighting with a large softbox as key light, a fill reflector to soften shadows, and a subtle hair light for separation. The subject should be framed from the chest up with sharp focus on the eyes and face, preserving natural skin tones and texture without over-smoothing. The final image should look like a real DSLR photograph taken by a professional corporate photographer, suitable for company websites, email signatures, and business cards.
LinkedIn Professional Photo
Formal · Clean
Generate a clean LinkedIn profile picture with the subject wearing formal business attire — a well-tailored dark blazer over a light blue dress shirt with the top button open for an approachable yet professional look. The expression should be confident and warm, with a natural slight smile that conveys trustworthiness and competence. Soft, diffused studio lighting from a large umbrella creates even, flattering illumination across the face without harsh shadows. The background is a smooth, clean gradient from medium grey to lighter grey, creating a timeless corporate aesthetic. The image should be high-resolution and perfectly composed for the LinkedIn profile picture circle crop.
Modern Office Portrait
Professional · Natural
Create a professional office headshot with the subject positioned in a modern open-plan workspace. The background shows a tastefully blurred office environment with soft bokeh of computer monitors, indoor plants, and warm ambient lighting, creating depth and context without distraction. The subject is in sharp focus wearing business casual attire with balanced, natural-looking lighting that combines overhead office fluorescents with window light. Colors are realistic and true-to-life with neutral white balance. The composition frames the subject from mid-chest up, slightly off-center with the office environment providing professional context, ideal for team pages and company intranets.
Executive CEO Style
Premium · Formal
A corporate executive portrait featuring the subject in a dark tailored suit with a silk tie, photographed with dramatic studio lighting that creates bold shadows and highlights reminiscent of a premium business magazine cover shoot. A single key light from the upper left carves out the bone structure while a subtle fill prevents the shadows from going completely dark. The background is a deep, rich charcoal that fades to near-black. The image has a cinematic color grade with warm skin tones against cool shadows, creating a powerful sense of authority and success. Shot with a 85mm lens at f/2 for beautiful subject isolation and gallery-quality sharpness.
Resume Profile Photo
Simple · Formal
A professional resume headshot with the subject wearing crisp formal attire — a well-pressed white dress shirt beneath a classic navy blazer. The background is a clean, seamless white or very light grey with no gradients or textures, meeting standard resume photo requirements across industries. The subject maintains perfect formal posture with shoulders squared to the camera and chin slightly elevated for a confident, competent appearance. Soft studio lighting is perfectly balanced to illuminate both sides of the face evenly with no distracting shadows. The image is captured with razor-sharp facial clarity, clear enough to print at high resolution without any loss of detail.
Business Casual Headshot
Friendly · Professional
A warm and inviting business casual profile picture featuring the subject in smart-casual attire — a fitted button-down shirt with rolled sleeves or a clean polo shirt in a muted, pleasant color. The subject displays a relaxed, genuine smile that conveys approachability and friendliness while maintaining professionalism. Soft, enveloping studio light from a large diffused source creates a bright, airy feel with minimal shadows and a fresh, modern aesthetic. The background is a clean, neutral tone that complements the outfit without competing for attention. The overall image feels contemporary and personable, perfect for startups, creative agencies, and customer-facing profiles.
Premium Corporate Portrait
Luxury · Branding
A high-end corporate portrait that exudes premium quality and executive presence. The subject is posed with confident body language — arms subtly crossed or one hand on a lapel — wearing a bespoke suit with visible attention to tailoring details. The background features an out-of-focus luxury office environment with rich wood paneling, leather furniture, and warm accent lighting that speaks to success and achievement. Every detail in the image is rendered with crisp, magazine-quality clarity, from the texture of the suit fabric to the individual hairs of perfectly groomed brows. The color palette is rich and warm with deep browns, warm skin tones, and subtle golden highlights.
Minimal Professional Headshot
Modern · Clean
A minimal, modern headshot with a stripped-back aesthetic that puts all emphasis on the face. The subject is photographed against a simple, solid neutral background — pure white, light grey, or soft beige — with zero distractions or visual noise. Natural lighting from a large window provides soft, directional illumination that creates gentle modeling on the face with beautiful, organic shadows. The composition is a tight head-and-shoulders crop with the face occupying at least 60% of the frame. The image has a clean, contemporary feel with natural colors and no heavy post-processing, embodying the 'less is more' philosophy of modern minimalist design.
Cinematic Professional Portrait
Polished · Dramatic
A professional cinematic headshot that elevates a standard corporate photo into an artistic statement. The shallow depth of field at f/1.4 creates a silky smooth background blur that makes the subject appear to float against an ethereal wash of muted colors. Subtle dramatic lighting uses a slightly lower key ratio than typical corporate photography, adding Rembrandt-style shadows that create depth and visual intrigue while remaining workplace-appropriate. The subject's expression is confident and compelling, photographed at a slight three-quarter angle for dynamic composition. The image receives a polished corporate finish with refined color grading — slightly lifted blacks, warm midtones, and crisp highlights — resulting in a headshot that is simultaneously professional and artistically distinctive.
